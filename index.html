Chapter 1: The Rise of BNB

BNB began as a simple utility token on Binance Exchange. Few realized CZ had bigger plans.

In 2020, Binance launched Binance Smart Chain (BSC) to support smart contracts with fast transactions and low fees, making it an attractive alternative to Ethereum.

As DeFi exploded, BSC quickly became a dominant force for yield farms, DEXs, and innovative projects. But its true strength emerged as the go-to chain for Memecoins, offering a low-cost playground while Ethereum faced rising gas fees.

By 2021, BNB had established itself as the undisputed king of crypto — dominating both DeFi and the Memecoin landscape. But what goes up must come down…
4

Chapter 2: The Bear Market and Solana's Rise

Then came the great bear market of 2022.

The entire crypto market entered a prolonged phase of stagnation. BNB, once at the forefront, became irrelevant as trading volume evaporated.

During this bleak period, a new star emerged: Solana. It redefined meme trading by offering:

✅ Incredibly fast transactions

✅ Extremely low fees

✅ A vibrant ecosystem for viral tokens

As new capital cautiously returned to crypto, it flooded into Solana instead of BNB. Solana emerged as the new Memecoin powerhouse — while BNB remained forgotten.
5

Chapter 3: Solana's Collapse

What started as a promising era quickly spiraled into chaos. Pump.fun emerged on Solana, allowing anyone to create Memecoins in seconds with no coding skills required.

But success came at a cost. Solana's technical instability became critical:

• Repeated network outages at crucial moments

• Infrastructure repeatedly collapsed under pressure

• Memecoins launched and rugged faster than anyone could track

The tipping point came with Trump's Memecoin launch, which overloaded Solana's network. Transactions froze, liquidity vanished, and countless traders lost investments.

Meanwhile, BSC stood firm, maintaining stability and showcasing superior reliability.
6

Chapter 4: CZ's Masterstroke – BSC’s Comeback

CZ repositioned BSC as the superior choice:

✅ Faster than Ethereum

✅ Better than Solana

✅ Proven stability

He reignited Memecoin hype by:

- Launching Four Meme, Binance’s Pump.fun rival

- Strengthening security

- Marketing BSC as the “Home of Memecoins”

- Partnering with influencers & trading communities

Binance backed key Memecoins like Broccoli Meme, which gained enough traction to land on Binance Futures.
